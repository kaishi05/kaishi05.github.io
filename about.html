About Me

    

 

I am Likun, a California licensed landscape architect with more than 15-year experience in this field. With academic background in architecture and landscape design, I believe high quality residential landscaping not only encourages your outdoor “rooms” usage, but also inspires the feeling of your own home and your connection with nature. My working goal is to offer my clients the best design to build beautiful garden in your property so you and your family can enjoy and relax of the daily stress. To achieve this goal, I am diving into work with passion and professional experience. When I am not working, I enjoy landscape sketching and spending time with my families.
